Fajitas

Description

An easy and healthy weeknight meal that comes together within 20 mins.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Spices of choice
  • Small corn tortillas

Steps

  1. Saute onion and peppers in a pan with oil
  2. Set aside
  3. Cook chicken pieces until internal temp is 165
  4. Heat tortillas in microwave or pan
  5. Assemble with tortillas, chicken, and veggie mix
