Teryaki Bowls

Description

A perfect meal when you have an asian food craving, with limited ingredients and packed with nutrients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast or thigh
  • Broccoli (or veggie of choice)
  • Teryaki sauce
  • Spices of choice
  • White rice

Steps

  1. Cook rice according to package
  2. While rice is cooking, saute chicken pieces in pan
  3. Toss cooked chicken in teryaki sauce
  4. Cook veggie of choice to your preference
  5. Assemble with rice, chicken, and veggie
