Lemon Pasta

Description

Light lemony pasta that easily pairs with chicken or shrimp and a lovely white wine.

Easy to pull together in just 30 mins, this pasta includes little ingredients you might already have.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Juice of a lemon
  • Zest of a lemon
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesean
  • Garlic
  • Shallot
  • Fresh parsley
